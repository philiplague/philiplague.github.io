About Me

Philip Lague

Philip Lague is a Senior from Colchester, Vermont who attended the Computer Animation & Web Design program at Center for Technology, Essex. He focused on learning Web Design, Video Editing and 3D Modeling/Animation. He is interested in Game Design and Sound Design. He plans to take a gap year to figure out his future career path. In Philip’s spare time, he plays video games, alone and with friends, competitively and casually. He also enjoys time with family, sings, plays and listens to music, explores different foods from around the world, and learns new things. Philip earned the CWDSA Associate Certification from WebProfessionals and is a competent web designer.
